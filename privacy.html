Privacy Policy

At Ember, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. What We Collect

We collect basic user data such as Discord user IDs, server IDs, and moderation logs necessary for core bot functions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data only for moderation, protection, and analytics within your server. We do not sell or share your data.

3. Data Retention

Your data is stored securely and only retained for as long as your server uses Ember.

4. Contact

If you have questions about your data or this policy, contact our team via our support server.
